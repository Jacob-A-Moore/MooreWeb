About Me

I am a driven technology professional with a deep passion for learning and continuously expanding my skillset. Since 2021, I’ve immersed myself in popular programming languages like C# and Python, alongside mastering frameworks such as .NET, ASP.NET, Django, and Tailwind CSS. My journey into software engineering began with a lifelong love for video games, sparking my curiosity in how technology works. Even though my focus is not video game development, this passion fuels my commitment to staying current with new technologies and honing my craft to build innovative solutions.

{% load static %}

My introduction to working with computers began with my initial introduction to circuitry and binary logic through the Tennessee College of Applied Technology certification program in Industrial Electricity. This program taught me the basics of circuitry, like Voltage, Resistance and Amperage as well as Impedence and Reactance. Upon mastering these basic concepts I was introduced to control logic wiring which exposed me to the concept of combinational logic. The intricacies of PLC's and the circuitry involved in wiring input and outputs to perform specific tasks in a particular order led me into doing research into Computer Science.

Once I zeroed in on Software Development within the vast IT universe, I enrolled in Chattanooga State’s AAS program in Computer Programming, and my journey into the world of coding truly began. From my first “Hello World!” in Java and C#, I was hooked. That curiosity soon evolved into building a full-scale library management system with a team. My education not only sharpened my technical abilities but also taught me how to thrive in team environments. Today, I continue honing my skills, both in programming and in working as part of a team—because coding is rarely a solo sport.
